EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between Cedarline Technologies Pvt. Ltd. and the
Employee named below, effective April 1, 2026.

Employee Name: [Employee Name]
Job Title: Senior Software Engineer
Salary: PKR 350,000 per month, payable monthly in arrears

1. DUTIES
The Employee shall perform duties as assigned by the Employer, including software development,
code review, and mentoring of junior engineers. The Employee agrees to devote full working hours
to the Employer's business.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee shall not disclose any confidential information, trade secrets, or proprietary data
belonging to the Employer, both during and after employment.

3. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty days written notice. The Employer may
terminate immediately for cause, including misconduct or breach of company policy.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, and code created by the Employee during the course of employment
shall be the sole property of the Employer.

5. BENEFITS
The Employee shall be entitled to twenty days of paid annual leave, medical insurance coverage,
and provident fund contributions as per company policy.

6. NON-COMPETE
The Employee agrees not to work for a direct competitor of the Employer within Pakistan for a
period of six months following termination of employment.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
